About Tech Mentors

History

'Tech Mentors' online higher study institute established in 2016 as a solution for working and other people to fulfil their academic qualifications in different sectors with distance educational options. Due to some various reasons students are unable to visit and learn physically with time restrictions. Although every student wants to do their higher studies to achieve different goals in their career path. Then Tech Mentors is here to support their career dream in online and distant mode. We have started Tech Mentors with few students and few lecturers for a simply fine course fee. From the beginning to the present our institute had taken a huge progress as an online higher study provider. Nowadays we are in a good competition with other institutes and a challenged to them. This is a good commitment to students to achieve thei dreams through the career path.
In todays Tech Mentors become a leading higher studies provider in the island with over 5,000 students and over 50 lecturers. This is the best online platform to do your higher studies.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and stabled online higher study institute in Sri Lanka.

Our Mision

Provide students each & every opportunity to achieve their qualifications in international level.
